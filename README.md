# springtheory

Gaussian inference as mechanics: demos and education for a Hookean universe.

**Site:** [springtheory.microprediction.org](https://springtheory.microprediction.org) (served from `docs/`)

Precision is mass, energy is negative log-likelihood, force is the score. Evidence
fuses at the centre of mass (masses in parallel add); noise compounds through
channels by the reduced-mass rule (springs in series combine harmonically); the
Kalman filter alternates the two. Loosely based on
[The Analog Kalman Filter](https://medium.com/geekculture/the-analog-kalman-filter-e1f5bbba2f7f).

## Contents

- `docs/index.html` — the pitch, and the reading list
- `docs/intro.html` — Hooke's law to Bayes' rule in five consolidation moves
- `docs/dictionary.html` — the mechanics ↔ statistics translation table
- `docs/demos.html` — interactive demos (evidence fusion, reduced mass, least
  squares as springs, the time-series smoother, bid-ask bounce)

Demos are single-file HTML + canvas, no build step.

Issues and demo ideas welcome.
